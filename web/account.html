Get your API key

Sign with Phantom to identify your wallet. We'll give you an API key that authenticates you in Claude / Cursor — no email, no password.

1
Connect your Phantom wallet (the one you'll use with autoyield).
2
Sign a free, off-chain message proving you own the wallet. No on-chain transaction. No fees.
3
Copy the API key shown — paste it into your MCP config as Authorization: Bearer <key>.

Lost your key? Just sign in again — we'll mint a new one. Old keys keep working until you ask us to revoke them (coming soon).
