autoyield

Non-custodial RWA + yield service for AI agents on Solana.

Solana mainnet MCP-native Funds in your wallet pre-alpha

⚠ Pre-alpha: limited testing on real funds. Not for U.S. persons. Read the disclaimer before signing anything.

Connect this MCP to Claude, Cursor, or any MCP-compatible AI. Your AI can then compare on-chain yields, quote tokenized US equities, and build ready-to-sign transactions. You sign every action in your own Phantom wallet — we never custody.

What it does

  • Risk-adjusted yield ranking across Solana / Ethereum / Base / Arbitrum — not headline APY, but APY discounted for volatility, TVL depth, protocol risk & reward-emission dependence. A safe 6% out-ranks a thin, predicted-to-fall 40%.
  • Buy / sell Backed xStocks (NVDAx, AAPLx, TSLAx, SPYx) via Jupiter — with a best-entry-timing signal: is the token rich or cheap vs the underlying stock right now?
  • Deposit USDC into Ondo USDY tokenized treasuries (~4–5% APY)
  • Portfolio health check — ask your AI "should I do anything?" It compares your holdings to the best risk-adjusted yield and flags rich/cheap xStock positions. A reason to act, or reassurance to hold.
  • Every action is a ready-to-sign transaction you approve in Phantom — in English or 中文. We never custody or sign.

Install

Add to ~/Library/Application Support/Claude/claude_desktop_config.json:

{
  "mcpServers": {
    "autoyield": {
      "command": "npx",
      "args": ["-y", "@kaditang/agent-wallet-mcp"]
    }
  }
}

Why this exists

Tokenized US treasuries (Ondo USDY, BlackRock BUIDL) and tokenized equities (Backed xStocks) became the fastest-growing RWA category in 2026. After the GENIUS / CLARITY Acts restricted stablecoin issuer interest, the cleanest yield path is direct ownership of these securities — but the infrastructure has been built for traders, not AI agents. autoyield wraps the boring-but-correct execution in a service your AI can drive.
